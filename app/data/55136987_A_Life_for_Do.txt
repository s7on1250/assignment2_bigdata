A Life for Do () is a 1954 West German drama film directed by Gustav Ucicky and starring Hans Söhnker, Paola Loew and Heidi Becker. It was shot at the Tempelhof Studios in West Berlin and on location around Lucerne and Zurich. The film's sets were designed by the art directors Emil Hasler and Walter Kutz. ==Plot== Do lives with her foster father on a farm in South Africa.

A Dangerous Fortune is a novel written by British author Ken Follett in 1993. The story is set against the backdrop of collapse of a bank in 1866. The book also features Follett's first female villain, the domineering and unscrupulous Augusta. == Summary == The prologue, set in 1866, depicts the day of the accident. It introduces young college students, the main characters that we find throughout the novel. It also positions the place of each character in society.

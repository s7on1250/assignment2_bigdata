A Drive into the Blue (German: Die Fahrt ins Blaue) is a 1919 German silent comedy film directed by Rudolf Biebrach and starring Henny Porten, Georg Alexander, and Jakob Tiedtke.Bock & Bergfelder p.372 It was shot at the Tempelhof Studios in Berlin.

A Heart Full of Music (German: Ein Herz voll Musik) is a 1955 West German musical romantic comedy film directed by Robert A. Stemmle and starring Vico Torriani, Ina Halley and Ruth Stephan.MacKenzie p.177Silberman & Wrage p.147 It was shot in Eastmancolor at the Bavaria Studios in Munich and on location in Rome and St. Moritz. The film's sets were designed by the art directors Hertha Hareiter and Otto Pischinger. ==Cast== * Vico Torriani as Vico Hasenpfot * Ina Halley as Blanche Lichtli * Ruth Stephan as Fleurette * Wolfgang Wahl as Peer Saldo * Fita Benkhoff as Ellinor Patton * Boy Gobert as Granito Bubiblanca * Annemarie Sauerweinas Liliane Hasenpfot * Paul Bildt as Lichtli * Rudolf Vogel as Geschäftsführer Léaux * Mantovani as himself == References == ==Bibliography== * MacKenzie, Colin.

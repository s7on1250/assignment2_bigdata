A Gershwin Fantasia is a single by jazz pianist Mike Garson, and was released in 1992. The lone track, "A Gershwin Fantasia" is a medley of tracks by George Gershwin, given an avant-garde treatment by Garson. In 2008 the track was combined with the Oxnard Sessions, Vol.2 and re-released as the album 'Jazzhat' by Allegro Records. ==Track listing== ==External links== * Jazz Hat review.

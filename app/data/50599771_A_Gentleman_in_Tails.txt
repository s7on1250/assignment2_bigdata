A Gentleman in Tails (Spanish:Un caballero de frac) is a 1931 American comedy film directed by Roger Capellani and Carlos San Martín and starring Roberto Rey, Gloria Guzmán and Rosita Díaz Gimeno.Peiró p.302 It is the Spanish- language version of the 1927 film Evening Clothes. It was made by Paramount Pictures at the Joinville Studios in Paris.

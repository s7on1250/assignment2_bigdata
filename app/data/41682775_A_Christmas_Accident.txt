A Christmas Accident is a 1912 American Christmas film. Prints and/or fragments of the film were found in the Dawson Film Find in 1978. == Story == Two families live next door in the same house. The Biltons have many children and strive to make the ends meet. The Giltons are well to do. Mr. Gilton is a grumpy old man, who gets annoyed by the children. He accuses the Biltons of having poisoned his dog. On Christmas Eve Mr. Gilton accidentally bumps into the Bilton home, and he is overwhelmed when one of the children gives him her present. == Cast == *William Wadsworth - Mr. Gilton *Mrs. William Bechtel - Mr. Gilton's wife *Augustus Phillips - Mr. Bilton *Ida Williams - Mr. Bilton's wife *Edna Hammel - Cora Cordelia Bilton ==See also== * List of Christmas films == References == == Further reading == * * == External links == * * Category:American Christmas drama films Category:American silent short films Category:American black-and-white films Category:1911 films Category:1910s Christmas drama films Category:1911 short films Category:1911 drama films Category:1912 drama films Category:1912 films Category:1910s English-language films Category:1910s American films Category:Silent American drama films

A Great Coup is a 1919 British silent sports film directed by George Dewhurst and Walter West and starring Stewart Rome, Poppy Wyndham and Gregory Scott. It was based on a novel by Nat Gould.Low p.152 The film is about a racehorse owner who decides to race his best horse in a major race meeting after his jockey is nobbled by the opposition. ==Cast== * Stewart Rome - Squire Hampton * Poppy Wyndham - Kate Hampton * Gregory Scott - Reid Gordon * Cameron Carr - Richard Foxton ==References== ==Bibliography== * Low, Rachael.

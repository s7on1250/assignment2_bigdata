A K M Siddiq, born Abul Khair Muhammad Siddiq, was an academic from Bangladesh who was the 18th Vice-Chancellor of Dhaka University. == Early life == Siddiq was a PhD from the University of Saskatchewan in Physics. He joined the University of Dhaka in 1950. == Career == Siddiq was the Vice-Chancellor of the University of Dhaka from 21 March 1983 to 16 August 1983. Siddiq was the Pro-Vice Chancellor of the University of Dhaka from 24 August 1981 to 22 August 1985. == Death == Siddiq died on 31 August 2018 in Gulshan, Dhaka, Bangladesh.

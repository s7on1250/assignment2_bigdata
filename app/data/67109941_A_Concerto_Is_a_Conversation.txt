A Concerto Is a Conversation is a 2021 American documentary film directed by Ben Proudfoot and Kris Bowers. ==Summary== The 13-minute film centers on Bowers's conversations with his jazz pianist grandfather about personal and family history.Matthew Carey, "Grandfather, Grandson: Two Generations Meet Face To Face In Oscar-Shortlisted ‘A Concerto Is A Conversation’".

A Fairer World Is Possible () is a non-fiction book by Turkish president Recep Tayyip Erdogan which was published in seven languages in 2021. == Topic == The book, which sets out with the doctrine "The world is bigger than 5", states that the global politics should not rest on a small minority of countries. The book touches on the duties of the United Nations for a sustainable and fair world. In the book, global problems such as injustice, corruption, the refugee crisis, the world's spectacle of what is happening in the Middle East, international terrorism and Islamophobia are addressed. == References == Category:Turkish books

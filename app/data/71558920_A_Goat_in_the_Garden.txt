A Goat in the Garden (Swedish: Bock i örtagård) is a 1958 Swedish comedy film directed by Gösta Folke and starring Edvin Adolphson, Gunnel Broström and Eva Stiberg. It was based on the 1933 novel of the same title by Fritiof Nilsson Piraten.Costello p.193 It was shot at the Råsunda Studios in Stockholm. The film's sets were designed by the art director P.A.

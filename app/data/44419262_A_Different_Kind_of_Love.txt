"A Different Kind of Love" is a song written by Joacim Dubbelman, Martin Landh and Sam McCarthy, and performed by Caroline Wennergren at Melodifestivalen 2005. Participating in the fourth semifinal in Växjö, the song made it directly to the finals, where it ended up 5th.

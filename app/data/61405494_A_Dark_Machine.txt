A Dark Machine is the second studio album by Australian producer ShockOne. Warner Music Australia and Canadian record label Monstercat released the album on 1 August 2019. ==Background== The album was announced through a tweet from Monstercat. The first single through the label, "Run" featuring Italian electronic music act The Bloody Beetroots, was released on 27 June 2019. A music video for the single was released on the same day.

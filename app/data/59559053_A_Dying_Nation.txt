A Dying Nation or Dying Peoples () is a 1922 German silent historical drama film directed by Robert Reinert and starring Paul Wegener, Otto Gebühr, and Fritz Kortner. It was released in two parts Heimat in Not and Brennendes Meer. The film's sets were designed by the art directors Kurt Dürnhöfer and Walter Reimann.

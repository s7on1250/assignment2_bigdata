A Journey Through Filmland is a 1921 documentary about Hollywood made by Beaumont Smith during his visit there. It includes appearances from Charlie Chaplin, Douglas Fairbanks, Warren Kerrigan, Mildred Harris, Ben Turpin, Wallace Reid and Bill Hart. ==References== Category:1921 films Category:Australian documentary films Category:Australian silent films Category:Australian black-and-white films Category:1921 documentary films Category:Documentary films about Hollywood, Los Angeles

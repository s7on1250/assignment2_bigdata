A Christmas Gift to You is the second Christmas album by Canadian country music artist Johnny Reid. It was released on October 22, 2013 by Universal Music Canada. The album was produced by Bob Ezrin. It features collaborations with Peter Frampton and Isabelle Boulay. It won the 2014 Juno Award for Adult Contemporary Album of the Year. ==Commercial performance== In its first week of release, the album debuted at number seven on the Canadian Albums Chart.

A Gift from Nessus is a novel by the Scottish writer William McIlvanney published in 1968 and republished in 2014. The novel in 1968 was awarded a Scottish Arts Council Book Award and it was adapted by Bill Craig for the BBC series Play for Today in 1980. ==References== Category:Scottish novels Category:1968 British novels Category:Novels by William McIlvanney Category:Eyre & Spottiswoode books

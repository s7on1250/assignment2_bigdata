"A Fool for You" is a bluesy, proto-soul single written and released by musician Ray Charles on Atlantic Records in 1955. The single was Charles' second number-one R&B; hit. ==Personnel== *Lead vocal and piano by Ray Charles *Instrumentation by the Ray Charles band *Produced by Jerry Wexler ==Covers== *Ike & Tina Turner on The Ike & Tina Turner Show (Vol. 2) *Stevie Wonder on I Was Made To Love Her *Otis Redding on The Immortal Otis Redding *Van Morrison on A Night in San Francisco *Van Morrison on The Healing Game *Michael Jackson on Soulsation! ==References== Category:1955 singles Category:1955 songs Category:Ray Charles songs Category:Songs written by Ray Charles Category:Atlantic Records singles Category:Song recordings produced by Jerry Wexler Category:Ike & Tina Turner songs Category:Stevie Wonder songs Category:Otis Redding songs

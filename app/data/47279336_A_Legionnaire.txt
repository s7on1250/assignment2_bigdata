A Legionnaire (French: Un de la légion) is a 1936 French comedy adventure film directed by Christian-Jaque and starring Fernandel, Robert Le Vigan and Daniel Mendaille.Andrews p.355 The film's sets were designed by the art director Pierre Schild. Location shooting took place in Marseille and Sidi Bel Abbès. ==Plot== A hen-pecked husband finds his life turned upside down, when he is accidentally enlisted in the French Foreign Legion.

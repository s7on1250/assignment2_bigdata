A Fellow on a Furlough is a World War II song written by Bobby Worth in 1943 and published by House of Melody. The chorus states, "He's just a fellow on a furlough / Whose hopes have all come true / The girl of his furlough dream is you."Holloway, Diane, and Bob Cheney. American History in Song: Lyrics from 1900 to 1945. San Jose: Authors Choice Press, 2001, P. 401, The song is performed by Bob Crosby in the 1944 film Meet Miss Bobby Socks. == References == === Bibliography === *Holloway, Diane, and Bob Cheney.

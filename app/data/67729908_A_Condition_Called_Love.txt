An anime television series adaptation is set to premiere in 2024. ==Media== ===Manga=== The series is written and illustrated by Megumi Morino. It premiered in the February 2018 issue of Dessert, which shipped on December 2, 2017. The first volume was released on May 11, 2018. As of June 2023, thirteen tankōbon volumes have been released. On March 8, 2020, Kodansha USA announced they would publish the series digitally. During their panel at Anime Expo 2022, Kodansha USA announced that they would begin publishing the manga in print in Q2 2023. ====Volume list==== ===Anime=== An anime television series adaptation was announced on June 7, 2023. It is set to premiere in 2024. ==Reception== The series was a nominee for the 64th Shogakukan Manga Awards in the shōjo category. It was also the runner-up for the ninth An An manga award. In 2018, employees of one of the largest Japanese bookstores ranked the series as the tenth best manga of 2018. The series ranked fifteenth in the 2020 Kono Manga ga Sugoi! guidebook's top manga for female readers. Also in 2020, it was nominated for the Kodansha Manga Award in the shōjo category. In 2021, it won the Kodansha Manga Award in the shōjo category. As of June 2023, the series has 3.8 million copies in circulation.

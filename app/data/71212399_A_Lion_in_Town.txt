A Lion in Town (Swedish: Lejon på stan) is a 1959 Swedish comedy film directed by Gösta Folke and starring Nils Poppe, Ann-Marie Gyllenspetz and Herman Ahlsell.Qvist & Von Bagh p.126 It was shot at the Råsunda Studios in Stockholm. The film's sets were designed by the art director Rolf Boman. ==Cast== * Nils Poppe as Charlie * Ann-Marie Gyllenspetz as Greta Berg * Herman Ahlsell as Carl-Adam Pettersson * Jan-Erik Lindqvist as Valdemar 'Walle' Blom * Sigge Fürst as Supt.

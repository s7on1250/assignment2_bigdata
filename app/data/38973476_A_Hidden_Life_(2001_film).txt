A Hidden Life () is a 2001 Brazilian drama film directed by Suzana Amaral. ==Cast== * Sabrina Greve as Biela * Eliane Giardini as Constança * Cacá Carvalho as Conrado * João Antônio as Dr. Godinho * Benício Aleixo Bernardo as Truco player * Neuza Borges as Joviana * Tânia Botelho as Dona Alice * Erasmo Xavier da Costa as Truco player 2 * João Luiz Pompeu de Pina as Cavaleiro 2 * Itamar Gonçalves as Gumercindo * Nayara Guércio as Mazília ==Reception== It was entered into the 24th Moscow International Film Festival. At the 34th Festival de Brasília, it won the Best Actress Award (Sabrina Greve) and Best Sound.

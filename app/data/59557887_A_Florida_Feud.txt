A Florida Feud is a 1909 American silent film produced by Kalem Company and directed by Sidney Olcott, shot in Florida. ==Production notes== The film was shot in Jacksonville, Florida. The first The Moving Picture World Vol 4, n°1, p. 11 of a series shot for Kalem during winter and spring.

A Farmer's Life () is a 1965 Danish comedy film directed by Erik Balling and starring Lone Hertz, Morten Grunwald and Ellen Winther Lembourn.Sundholm p.68 It is based on the classic German novel From My Farming Days by Fritz Reuter, and Fleming Lynge's operetta inspired by the novel.

A Gentleman Roughneck is a 1925 American silent action film directed by Grover Jones and starring Frank Merrill, Virginia Warwick and Jack Richardson.Munden p.285 The plot revolves around corruption in a lumber camp. ==Cast== * Frank Merrill * Virginia Warwick * William Conklin * Jack Richardson * Murdock MacQuarrie * William T.

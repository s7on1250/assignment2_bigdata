{{Infobox short story | name = A Collegiate Casting-Out of Devilish Devices | image = | caption = | author = Terry Pratchett | language = English | series = Discworld 5th short story | genre = Fantasy, Wizards and Academic administration ;Characters : The Wizards ;Locations : Unseen University | pub_date = | published_in = | publisher = | preceded_by_italics = | followed_by_italics = }} "A Collegiate Casting-Out of Devilish Devices" is a Discworld short story by Terry Pratchett. The story describes the reaction of the wizards of the Unseen University to a proposal from the Patrician to introduce regulation of university education. It references the government inspector A. E. Pessimal, who goes on to have a significant role in Thud! It was first published in the 13 May 2005 issue of The Times Higher Education Supplement, and can be found in certain editions of Snuff.

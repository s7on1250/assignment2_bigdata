A Girl from the Chorus () is a 1937 German comedy film directed by Carl Lamac and starring Anny Ondra, Viktor Staal and Ursula Grabley. It is set in the theatre world of Berlin around the turn of the twentieth century. It was shot at the Babelsberg Studios in Berlin.
